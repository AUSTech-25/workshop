Organizers and Biographies

Dr. Badri Narayan Subudhi

Associate Professor at IIT Jammu. His research includes underwater surveillance, statistical and neuromorphic models, image/video processing, and pattern recognition. He led major conferences such as NCC 2025 and NCVPRIPG 2025 and contributed to workshops at ICCV 2025 and ICPR 2024.

Dr. Arijit Sur

Professor at IIT Guwahati. His group focuses on deep learning in computer vision, covering image/video restoration, super-resolution, semantic segmentation, and more across underwater, satellite, and medical domains. He also works on zero-shot learning and adaptive video streaming in 5G.

Dr. Deepak Kumar Rout

Assistant Professor at IIIT Bhubaneswar. Expert in underwater object detection/tracking in complex environments and real-time recognition. His work spans robust AI-based perception systems and sensor integration.

Meghna Kapoor

PMRF research scholar at IIT Jammu. Her research in graph learning-based underwater surveillance contributes to environmental and autonomous systems. Reviewer for IEEE Access, CVPR, ICCV, and ACCV with several international grants and presentations.
