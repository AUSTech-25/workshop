Organizers and Biographies

Dr. Badri Narayan Subudhi

He is currently an Associate Professor with the Indian Institute of Technology Jammu, Jammu and Kashmir, India. His research includes underwater surveillance, statistical and neuromorphic models, image/video processing, and pattern recognition. He led major conferences such as NCC 2025 and NCVPRIPG 2025 and contributed to workshops at ICCV 2025 and ICPR 2024.

Dr. Arijit Sur

He is Professor at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati. His research is mainly focused on Deep Learning approaches to solving different Computer Vision problems like image, video restoration, super-resolution, semantic segmentation, and change detection in various application domains like underwater vision, satellite, and remote sensing image analysis, and medical imaging, media forensics, etc. His group is also working on different machine learning-based problems like zero-shot learning, semi-supervised learning, robotic vision, and ML-based approaches for adaptive video streaming in a 5G environment.

Dr. Deepak Kumar Rout

He is an Assistant Professor at IIIT Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. His research interests lie in underwater vision, particularly in the detection and tracking of objects under complex underwater conditions. He has contributed extensively to real-time object recognition and prominent object detection for underwater applications. His expertise spans AI-driven approaches for robust underwater perception and sensor-based monitoring systems.
