Keynote Speakers

Dr. Santosh Vipparthi

Dr. Santosh Vipparthi is an Associate Professor at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Engineering (sAIDE), IIT Ropar. He completed his B.E. from Andhra University, and M.Tech. & Ph.D. (with honors) from IIT Varanasi (BHU). He has previously served at IIT Guwahati and MNIT Jaipur.

His research focuses on visual perception, including object detection, underwater exploration, and affective computing. Dr. Vipparthi is known for leveraging deep learning for real-world challenges in vision and AI.
